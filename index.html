THE KINGDOM MADE OF CODE

Is a Sovreign state ruled by the The King Bunny first of his name. The Kingdom was founded in February 2023 and is a product of an Interactive design course at Beckmans Collage of Design. The Kingdom wants to be recognized as a Micronation of the Worldbut renounces any political positions.

As the kingdom still finds itself in its infancy,the current version is built with the simplest bricks of webcode and will transform with time.
